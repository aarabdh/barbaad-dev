---
title: 'Twenty-first Century'
date: '2020-06-01'
poem: true
---
Whisper whisper whisper <br><br/>
flutter flutter <br><br/>
On went the light <br><br/>
Off went the light <br><br/>
Callous cretins chirruped <br><br/>
Curtains curtsied curtly <br><br/>
The twenty first century

Fiends found follies <br><br/>
follies found fractures <br><br/>
Great giants gorged <br><br/>
glinting glamorous gems <br><br/>
Slimy slithery sunken <br><br/>
streets swelling slowly <br><br/>
The twenty first century

Rotten rugged rough rubble <br><br/>
Refugees ravaging roadside roughage <br><br/>
Rape rage rose <br><br/>
Roaring righteous resolutions <br><br/>
Ruthless rigidity <br><br/>
The twenty first century

Slender seductresses serving <br><br/>
Sluggish sinister strangers <br><br/>
Sulphurous smoke swallowing <br><br/>
Soldiers, servicemen, saints <br><br/>
Soaring slavery <br><br/>
The twenty first century

Questioning queers <br><br/>
Quires quoting Queens' quests <br><br/>
Quality quizzing quorums <br><br/>
Quite quiet, quick queues <br><br/>
Quippy quills quelling quandaries <br><br/>
The twenty first century.
