We are with you


17 June 2020

When the snow is black
And the rocks are red
Keep shooting, soldier
We are with you.

When the food is rationed
And the bunks are cramped
Think of your home, trooper
We are with you.

When the gear is too heavy
And the guns are half empty
Take our strength, warrior
We are with you.

When your blood boils the snow
And your steps shake the world
Don't stray your aim, fighter
We are with you.

When you hide behind the walls
And your hands are frozen stone
Don't lose hope, brother
We are with you.

When a bullet caresses your heart
And the night holds you in her arms
Take rest, martyr
We are with you.
